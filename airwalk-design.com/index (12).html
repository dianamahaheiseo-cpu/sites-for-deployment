Andar Bahar Rules

1. A joker card is drawn at the start of each round.

2. You can bet on either ANDAR (left) or BAHAR (right).

3. Cards are alternately dealt to ANDAR and BAHAR positions.

4. The game continues until a card matching the joker's value appears.

5. If the matching card appears on ANDAR, ANDAR bets win (1:1 payout).

6. If the matching card appears on BAHAR, BAHAR bets win (1:1 payout).

7. You can place multiple bets during the betting period.

8. The betting period lasts 15 seconds before cards are dealt.
